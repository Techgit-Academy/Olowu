ADDRESS OF HIS ROYAL MAJESTY,
OBA AHMED ADEKUNLE OYELUDE TEGBOSUN III DURING HIS OFFICIAL CORONATION AND
PRESENTATION OF STAFF OF OFFICE AND INSTRUMENT OF APPOINTMENT BY THE GOVERNOR OF THE STATE OF OSUN,
MR.RAUF ADESOJI AREGBESOLA ON SATURDAY, 15TH SEPTEMBER, 2012.


PROTOCOLS

The Governor of the State of Osun,
The Deputy Governor of the State of Osun,
Honourable Speaker, House of Assembly of Osun,
Honourable Members of the House of Assembly of Osun The Secretary to the State Government,

The Honourable Chief Judge,
His Royal Majesty The Ooni of Ife and other Royal Fathers presents,

Chieftains of Political Parties,
Gentlemen of the press,
Distinguished Guests,
Ladies and Gentlemen

I am indeed grateful to Almighty Allah for all his mercies and protection and for making it possible for me to ascend the throne of my forefathers. Today marks another milestone in the chequered history of Kuta. I welcome all our sons and daughters, friends, well wishers from all walks of life who have come to witness this memorable occasion and to rejoice with us.

Our profound appreciation goes to the Governor of the State of Osun, Ogbeni Rauf Adesoji Aregbesola for his fairness and objectivity in conducting the affairs of the state including chieftaincy matters. I equally appreciate religious leaders of the diverse faiths for their fervent and continuous prayers that have sustained us all in Kuta and the state as well as the country.

Mention must be made of the Kingmakers for their yeomanâ€™s jobin ensuring a smooth selection process which did not compromise the peace, unity and cohesion of our ancient town. The Ruling Houses and the other contestants are equally commended for their further spirit of sportsmanship. I extend my hand of fellowship to them to join hands with me in lifting our town to greater heights.

Kindly permit me to reiterate the fact that the institution of obaship remains an important historical and cultural heritage of the Yoruba people and partners in progress for government. It therefore, behoves all and sundry to continue to accord Obas respect in their domains as traditional rulers, fathers, spiritual leaders and custodians of societal culture and moral values. They will no doubt continue to uphold the dignity of the traditional institution.

The roles of traditional rulers as the custodians of traditions in their domains cannot be over- emphasized. They should therefore, be saddled with greater responsibilities in governance and development issues by all tiers of government. It is my candid opinion that the issue of constitutional roles for traditional rulers should be in the front burner because of its attendant benefit. I wish to commend the government at the centre for the transformational agenda. I wish to enjoin the Federal Government that the issue of insecurity should be accorded priority and addressed timely and promptly so as to fulfill the primary essence of governance. I have also noted the zeal, passion and enthusiasm with which the government of the State of Osun, under the leadership of Ogbeni Rauf Aregbe sola is implementing programmes contained in the 6-point Integral Action Plan. We will continue to support the laudable programmes aimed at engendering a better state and communities for all of us.

I will also use this opportunity to appeal to the government to consider my community in the schaeme of things while we pledge total support for the administration. The Ayedire Local Government led by the Executive Secretary, Mr. Abidemi Ogunkanmi is equally commended. My community will also continue to partner with the grassroot government, which is the nearest to the people to achieve success.

The Osun Council of Obas and Chiefs, under the leadership of His Imperial Majesty, Oba Okunade Sijuwade, Olubuse II which has accepted me gladly to its fold is also commended and I promise to join the team efforts at offering advice to all our sons and daughters at the commanding heights of governance, private sector and commence to engender commendable service. I wish to appeal to the good people of Kuta for their cooperation in my bid to give the town a new lease of life and effective leadership.

I implore you to spstain the spirit of loyalty, mutual trust, tolerance, understanding and patriotism as these are essential ingredients for the overall progress and, development of this community. I will equally enjoin all my subjects to be law-abiding and co-exist peacefully with one another as no meaningful development can be achieved in an atmosphere of rancor and acrimony.

Thank you and God Bless.
