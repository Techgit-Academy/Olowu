• IBB'S CONGRATULAORY MESSAGE



17th May, 2012


Kabiyesi Adekunle MAKAMA,



The news of your appointment as the new ruler of your kingdom did not come to me as a surprise. I had watched you closely all these years and became convinced beyond any iota of doubt that you are following the footsteps of your ancestors and would therefore take no time before you are ultimately called to serve. How I guess right.

True, Goldfish has no hidden place! Your people saw those unique qualities which are essential elements of genuine, true and quintessential statesmen in you. Little wonder you became their choice ofa lifetime leader. Aleader to be reverred, now and always: my Kabiyesi!

Our long years of friendship which was anchored on honesty, sincerity and undoubted loyalty is cherished and would always be cherished by me. I have no doubtthat yours shall be a succeed story.

In the coming months and years, | look forward to a pragmatic, honest, visionary, bold and kind hearted leader common of the Adekunle Makama I know. l am sure that you will do your people proud and would justify the ancestral trustand confidence reposed on you by them.

While we celebrate you today, it is imperative to note that; your experience, dexterity, exemplary stewardship coupled with your charismatic disposition singled you out to be so honoured.

Overtime, your general attitude to life reinforced my belief and conviction that given the right environment and opportunity, the sky shall be your limit. I am sure that you are abundantly endowed with all the requisite qualities a leader needs to successfully pilot his people towards the path of greatness.

| am also sure that using your network of friends and connection, you will propel your kingdom to an enviable position, and would apply your usual finesse to the socio-economic landscape of your kingdom for the greater good of all.

Your appointmentis indeed a well deserved one Kabiyesi, considering your aristocratic heritage and the highly rich resume acquired through long years of hard work, perseverance, commitment, focus, open heartedness and loyalty. No doubt these aforementiolled qualities pay, just it has done to you: my dearest Adekunle.

Your accessions to the throne of your ancestors came at a time when our beloved country is in dire need of experienced and hugely detribalized statesmen to provide responsible leadership to the citizens. Your diligence and passionate commitment to the overall growth and development of our country and its people cannot

be questioned in this regard. Again, I had no doubt that you will always bring to the table robust ideas, innovative and highly qualitative decisions capable of turning the fortunes of our people for good.

As you put the cap of your ancestors and ultimately start a new chapter in your life. I pray your reign brings prosperity, economic growth and development, and a guaranteed peace and security to your people and beyond.

May your reign be long and may it be full of all the good things of life.
Long live my dearest KabiyesiAdekunle MAKAMA.
