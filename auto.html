AUTOBIOGRAPHY OF OLOWU OF OWU KUTA



His Royal Highness Oba Hammed Adekunle Oyelude Tegbosun III, the Olowu of Kuta was born at Kuta by Prince Oyabayo Oyelude and Mrs.Serifatu Oyelude.

He was born into the Royal family of Tegbosun who was a direct son of Morolahun, the son ofAkindele Aniugbua founder of Kuta.

EDUCATION


Oba Hammed Adekunle Oyelude attended African Church Primary School Kuta for his elementary education.

He also attended African Church Secondary Modern School Kuta/lleogbo. He proceeded to Aldahunda Comprehensive College. Zaria between 1985-1989,

Institute of Information Technology (2004);

University of Maiduguri 1994.

He had the following Academic Certificates:

(a) Certificate in Structural Designs and Developments;
(b) Diploma (Integrated Structural Systems;
(c) B.Sc. Civil Engineering (2nd Class Lower)

PROFESSIONAL MEMBERSHIPS

Associate Member Nigeria Institute of Management;

Admitted Member, the WestAfrican Telecoms;
International Member, The Intemational Youth;
Member, African-African European Coop Oral:
Member, Nigeria Youths Organization:
Member, Civil Liberties Organization:
International Member, the International Youths Council; National Patron, Man O’War.

PERSONAL ATTRIBUTES

He has ability to work as aq team.
He has effective leadership qualities and has ability to move and work under pressure.
He is a fast leaner of new ideas, an achievement-driven professional and he is with the ability to Package a product to suit the needs or various audiences.

His vision is to be an Outstanding professional and world class Human and Material Resources Manager and Trainer.

His Publications And Research Works to Youths Development in a Challenging Economy:


“Stress Management’, An Exhaustive Training Manual to Reduce and Manage Individual and Corporate Stress. A researched Management Training Paper. Other manpower development Papers include “Business Communication”:
Work Environrnent Image “Image and Perception Management’, etc.

Since 2004, Oba Adekunle Oyelude has been a main facilitator at various Seminars and Conferences organized by different bodies and Organizations in Nigeria. He has also been a regular invitee to many Radios and Television Task shows on Environmental/Construction related issues and Youth issues.

EXPERIENCE: WORKING PROFILE

His working career has spanned the private sector of the Nigerian economy for he has gathered expeiences that cut across different strata of the society including; Aviation; Human and Material Resources Development and Management . He is also engaged in social works, expecially in the areas of Social Audit, Counselling and Leadership Training.

Oba Hammed Adekunle Oyelude was the Osun State Chairman of the Defunct United Nigeria Peoples Party, 2000-2003.

National Chairman: IBB Vision 2003-2007: presently Oba Adekiunle Oyelude is the Chief Executive Officer. Kings Road Constructions Limited, Chief Executive Officer, Palm Works Limited.

His hobbies include travelling, reading, meeting people and lawn tennis.

MARITAL STATUS

Oba Hammed Adekunle Oyelude is married and blessed with good children both male and female. His Olori, Alhaja Fatima Adeolalgbalaye has been very supportive in his ventures in life.

EXCEPTIONAL ATRIBUTE
